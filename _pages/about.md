I am a PhD student at the [Laboratory for Physical Chemistry](https://chab.ethz.ch/en/research/institutes/LPC.html) at [ETH Zurich](https://www.ethz.ch/en.html). I work in the research group of [Prof. Markus Reiher](http://reiher.ethz.ch/). I develop procedures to apply methods from the field of machine learning and artificial intelligence to theoretical chemistry. My first forthcoming paper applies Gaussian process regression and Bayesian sampling to dispersion interaction between organic compounds to yield more accurate results as well as streamline the pipeline of starting new reference calculations for compounds to dissimilar to the training set.
